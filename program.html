Our Program

Bilingual Instruction

English-Spanish guitar classes for elementary students, grades 3 and up. Parents welcome to attend and learn with their children.

Weekly Classes

One-hour classes twice per week. Flexible scheduling with enrollment exceptions for 2nd graders with siblings in the program.

Tablature Learning

Simplified learning using tablature method. Learn finger positioning, string selection, and chord progression step by step.

Performance Opportunities

Weekly in-class performances and two public concerts per semester. Students showcase their progress to families and school community.

Class Structure

  • One hour per class
  • Two classes per week
  • Grades 3 and up
  • 2nd grade exceptions for siblings
  • Parent participation welcome

Learning Method

  • Tablature-based instruction
  • Simplified chord learning
  • Finger positioning techniques
  • String selection practice
  • Ensemble playing

Practice Requirements

  • 10-15 minutes daily practice
  • Finger exercises
  • Tablature reading
  • Song review and practice
  • Family performances

Evaluation & Performance

  • Weekly individual performances
  • Weekly group performances
  • Two public concerts per semester
  • Progress tracking
  • School community showcases
