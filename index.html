About Our Program

The class offers bi-lingual (English-Spanish) guitar instruction to elementary school students, grades 3 and up. Classes meet for one hour each week, twice a week. Enrollment exceptions are made for 2nd grade participants who have siblings in the program.

Fundamental instruction includes: introduction to the guitar, its parts, care and treatment of the instrument, tuning, ergonomics and finally, playing the guitar.
